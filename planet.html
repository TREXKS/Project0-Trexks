THE PLANET

Here we address the major targets for Vertical Gardening

Automated Indoor Farm Production Horticultural Research Health and Wellness Waste and Sustainability Jobs
Effectively monitoring within an indoor system will log all resources that are accounted for and the plant receives exactly what it needs in order to thrive. Nothing is wasted. Everything is recycled.
We have established an ever growing open source system that allows for contributors to add their own development and style if needed. Our reasoning is not only just to facilitate a greener expansion within our cities and communities, but to encourage people from all around the world that it is in their best interest to make the change and recycle.
The beautiful thing about the relationship between plants and humans is that it is completely natural. We live off each other and supply each other with the resources to live a long and happy life. Through respiration and photosynthesis, we are able to transfer carbon dioxide and oxygen with each other supplying the other with its essential resources it needs to maintain its existence. As this fact holds true, more research is being introduced that is putting a spotlight on the biophysiological effects our relationship has on our well being.
At the end of the day, sustainability is the end all be all. Being able to maintain and regulate an environment for success is the most optimal situation and with that comes a lot of hard work and a willingness to put our egos aside and embrace change. Through our current methods, research tells us that if we do not change, our demise will be closer than we may think.
As the field and development progress, opportunity in each has its advantages and will grow.
