Mission

It is time to start making the world a bit greener. There is no debate that the world is changing due to our treatment and pollution of the planet, and if left untreated, the generations that follow will suffer.

Vertical Gardens is a project to promote the use of indoor gardens and hydroponic systems within popular cities. Population is growing, cities are becoming larger, and the need for resources is ever so increasing. Vertical Gardens lay out the technology and ideas in place to start facilitating construction of Vertical Gardens and what will need to be done to make it a reality.

About

Vertical Gardens is a exploratory idea to start conversation and networking. There is no set team other then the contributors to the community.
