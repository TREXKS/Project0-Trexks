Vertical Gardens

The Vertical Gardens project is a green energy initiative designed to integrate urban indoor gardening. The main goal is to globalize indoor gardening around the world and reduce the environmental impact outdoor farming and current stragedies have on the planet.

THE MISSION

The missions is simple. Clean the Earth! We are builders, innovators, and researchers whose mission is to brighten the future. Cities are some of the most populated areas in the world and contribute to environmental impact. Vertical Farms attemps to pave the way of integrating indoor farming in cities to give back to the community and use resources and technology we have to avoid serious impact on the climate.

CURRENT PROJECTS

Research investigating Hydroponic and Aeroponic systems using gardening technology for automated production. Sensors provide users information regarding quality control and nutrient selection. Templates for specific crops.

Milestone aimed to address psychological and physical effects working alongside neighboring plant life. Using medical tools to assess and anlyze biological responses within set environments.

Highlighting techniques and methods used for optimized waste management. Using waste for energy forms, amount needed, and ways to regulate.
